"BC2hell" is a project of the course "Design Thinking"

The goal of the course was to increase the screening rate of breast cancer using Design Thinking. BC stands for breast cancer, we want to send breast cancer to hell and that's the reason for the name.

Emphasize

make interviews with both breast cancer patients and women who haven't done breast cancer screening in order to have some insights.


Define Persona

Having a specific persona helps us define problem


Prototype

After some discussion, our team came up with the prototype together.
It is an official line (like WhatsApp in Taiwan) account that spread information to educate people with correct knowledge



Result

We've tracked how the knowledge spread and proves that this method does influence people
